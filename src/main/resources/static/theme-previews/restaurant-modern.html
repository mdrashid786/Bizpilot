Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Cooked with Passion

Tandoor & Thyme was founded in 2015 by a family of chefs who wanted to bring authentic regional Indian recipes — passed down for generations — to the modern table, without losing their soul.

Our tandoor is fired the traditional way, our spices are ground fresh daily, and every gravy is simmered for hours, not minutes. This is a kitchen that respects the craft of Indian cooking.
